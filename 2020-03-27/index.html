I have a theory

March 27, 2020

I have a theory that the more acroynms the government introduces, the worse the state of economy. Today our deputy prime minister laid out a new budget to support the economy during the pandemic. In his speech, he introduced 3 new acronyms:

  • Enhanced Jobs Support Scheme ("JSS")
  • Enhanced Wage Credit Scheme ("WCS")
  • Self-Employed Persons Income Relief Scheme ("SIRS")

Are 3 acronyms enough to reverse a recession?
