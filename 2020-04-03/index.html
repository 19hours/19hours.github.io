Splira.com

April 3, 2020

Originally, Splira.com was going to be my new project management tool but I never had the chance to finish it. I imagined it to be like a simpler version of Jira with a craiglist design. Little javascript, no silly frameworks. Fast and blazing. And I needed a domain that was short and sayable and a .com so I picked Splira.com. I was lucky enough to buy it with a coupon which came up to about 8 dollars. Maybe I'll continue to work on the site. For now though it's the new address of my internet space.
